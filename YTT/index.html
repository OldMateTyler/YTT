About Us

Your Talent Team is a family-oriented dance company that provides the foundation for aspiring dancers to develop their skills in a loving and encouraging environment. It is here where students are able to create life-long friendships with peers and teachers, whilst also achieving their most ambitious goals. Furthermore, through our dedicated team, students are provided with a platform to develop supplementary life-skills such as discipline, teamwork, and responsibility in a nurturing environment, free of consequences.Hello

Holiday Clinics


Holiday clinics are solely organised to give children of the Shoalhaven something to look forward to during their time off school.


These clinics are uniquely themed by staff at YTT and do no require any dance experience.

Running from 9am-3pm, the kids will be involved in countless activities that will ensure a great day outside of the house.


By signing your child up to one of these clinics, they will be able to connect with several children their age and provide you with a massive break.
